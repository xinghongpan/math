About Me

I am now an Associate Fellow at Scool of Mathematics, NUAA.
I received my Ph.D. in March 2017 at the Department of Mathematics in Nanjing University, and my advisor is Prof. Huicheng Yin. During 2013.01-2013.03, I was a visiting student at the University of California, Riverside. From December 2014 to December 2016, I was a joint PHD student at University of California, Riverside. In April 2017, I joined the NUAA as a Lecture.
